# TrainAI - AI Personal Trainer App

Personal trainers are expensive ($100+ multiple times per week). Imagine having your own personal trainer in your pocket on your device that knows your vitals, how you sleep, eat, and can tailor workout, fitness, and recovery plans to your body and to help you achieve your goals. 

## 🎯 Project Overview

TrainAI is a demonstration of how AI can provide truly personalized fitness coaching at scale. The app:

- **Tracks vital signs**: Sleep, energy, nutrition, pain levels
- **Adapts in real-time**: Adjusts workout intensity based on your current state
- **Provides complete guidance**: Workouts, nutrition plans, recovery recommendations
- **Uses cost-effective AI**: Built on Claude Haiku ($0.80 per 1M tokens vs $15 for Opus)

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Open the App
Simply open `TrainAI_App.html` in any web browser. No installation needed.

**Features:**
- **Home Screen**: View vital stats, weekly progress, recovery status
- **Vitals Update**: Input sleep, energy, steps, pain, nutrition
- **Training Plan**: See personalized workout with real-time adjustments based on vitals

### Example Adaptation
- **4 hours sleep** → Reduces workout volume by 20%, adds extra recovery
- **High energy** → Increases intensity and weight recommendations
- **Low energy** → Switches to lighter, mobility-focused session

## 📱 App Features

### Home Screen
- Vital signs dashboard (sleep, steps, workouts, recovery)
- Weekly progress tracking
- Real-time alerts for low sleep/recovery
- Quick access to update vitals or view plan

### Vitals Update Form
- Interactive sleep slider (0-12 hours)
- Energy level selector
- Pain/discomfort notes
- Nutrition quality tracking
- Real-time impact display showing how vitals affect your plan

### Training Plan Screen
- Today's personalized workout with adjustments
- Exercise-by-exercise breakdown with sets, reps, and form cues
- Original vs. modified comparison
- Recovery work recommendations
- Nutrition targets for the day

## 💡 How It Works

1. **Input Your Vitals** - Tell the app how you slept, your energy, nutrition, any pain
2. **AI Analyzes** - Claude Haiku instantly processes your data
3. **Plan Adjusts** - Your workout is modified in real-time based on your state
4. **Execute** - Follow your personalized training plan
5. **Recover** - Get recovery recommendations tailored to your vitals
